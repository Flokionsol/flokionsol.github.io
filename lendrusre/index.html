US Real Estate Stablecoin

US Real Estate Stablecoin

  • LendrUSRE is a stablecoin pegged to the US real estate market. 
  • USRE earns the same returns as the US real estate market and additional returns from DeFi lending.
  • USRE is a stablecoin, unaffected by bear markets.

The US real estate market is more than 40x larger than the total crypto market.

0% Interest Loans

0% Interest Loans

  • Users can create LendrUSRE by opening a loan (trove) with 0% interest and no deadline to return the loan. 
Earn With DeFi Lending Rewards

DeFi Lending Rewards

  • Lendr allows users to earn money through our defi lending platform.
  • 100% of fees from the lending platform are sent to stakers (and front end operators).
  • Stakers can withdraw at any time. There are no fees to stake or unstake in any Lendr pool.
  • Start now with our quick start video guides!
Backed by 110%+ Collateral

110%+ Collateral Ratio

  • All Lendr stablecoins are backed by at least 110% on-chain collateral.

The rewards generated from those tokens will be reserved for altruistic purposes.

Portfolio Diversification
